About Me

This is the story of a 26-year-old girl from a modest family who has dreamed to be a software developer since she was 14 years old. She wanted to go to a high school with a Computer Science focus, but her parents wanted her to be a professional tennis player and decided to push her in that direction. That's when she moved to Spain, enrolled in a basic high school, and set out to become the next Maria Sharapova. That dream, however, was never realized, and at the age of 19, she moved on and made a significant change in her life by becoming a casino dealer. Four years later, when Covid19 hit, she decided to use her tennis skills to pursue her lifelong dream of becoming a software developer. She is now a sophomore at Tiffin University in the United States on an athletic scholarship, majoring in Computer Science with a double concentration in Software Development and Web Design. She is a prestigious student with a 4.00 GPA, is trilingual in Spanish, Romanian, and English, and has a strong desire to learn more about programming languages.
